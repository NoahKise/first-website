Bloody Mary

This is a classic brunch cocktail

This recipe makes a pitcher that serves 20

What you'll need to make the Mix

  • 96 oz tomato juice
  • 2 tablespoons tamari
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons ginger syrup
  • 2 tablespoons tabasco
  • juice of 1 lime
  • heaping tablespoon spicy brown mustard
  • 30 cranks black pepper

Whisk together to combine

What you'll need for the cocktail

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 6 oz Bloody Mary mix
  • ice
  • celery salt (for rimming)
  • 1 celery stalk
  • any other optional garnishes (lime, lemon, olives, pickles, etc.)

Building the cocktail

  1. Use a lime wedge to moisten the rim of a pint glass
  2. Pour celery salt onto a plate and dip the rim of the glass into it
  3. fill the glass 3/4 full of ice
  4. pour in 2 oz vodka
  5. pour in 6 oz bloody mary mix
  6. use a celery stalk to mix drink, leave stalk in
  7. place optional garnishes on rim using toothpicks
  8. top with a crank of fresh black pepper

Enjoy and repeat as necessary until desired morning intoxication level is reached
